About Celtic Tours

This web site was created to allow you to search any city within the British Isles(inculding Ireland) for hotels, resturants, bars and site seeing opportunities at that Location. Weather you would like to plan a route along the alantic way in Ireland, or through the valley's of Wales or a weekend shopping trip to London, this site can provide guidance on which hotels you would like to stay at, the best pub and resturants in the area as well as key site seeing opportunties. To use this site just follow the four simmple steps.

  1. Firstly choose a country from the dropdown list. The map will then zoom directly over your desired country.
  2. Secondly choose one the type of venues you are looking for ie. Hotel, Bars, Resturants or site seeing opportunites
  3. Thirdly in the input box enter your destination. Note : If you orginally select Ireland from the drop down menu them you will only see cities in the the Republic of Ireland. If you choose one of the other countries then you will only be able to select cities that are part of the UK.(the Uk was the lowest level of granularity)
  4. Finally hit the submit button!

The results will be the map will zoom closer over your choosen destination and a plain icon on the center and depending on the option you choose, a number of different icones will display
